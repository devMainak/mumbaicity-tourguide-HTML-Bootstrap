Explore the Vibrancy of Mumbai City

Mumbai, formerly known as Bombay, is the financial, commercial, and entertainment capital of India. This city is known for its historical landmarks, busting markets, diverse cuisine, and lively events.

Immerse yourself i the rich history of Mumbai, visit iconic attractions, indulge in the local cuisine, and experience the city's vibrant events. Discover interesting facts and explore the convenient transportation options that make Mumbai truly unique.

Interesting Facts about Mumbai

  • Mumbai is home to the Bollywood film industry, one of the largest in the world.

  • The city is built on a series of reclaimed islands and has a robust coastal line.

  • Chhatrapati Shivaji Terminus (CST) is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and a prominent railway station.

  • Mumbai's local train network is one of the busiest in the world, connecting millions of commuters daily.

  • Gateway of India, an iconic monument, was built to commemorate the visit of King George V and Queen Mary to Mumbai.

Transportation in Mumbai

Mumbai offers a variety of transportation options, including:

  • Local Trains: The lifetime of Mumbai, connecting the city's suburban areas.

  • BEST Buses: The Brihanmumbai Electric Supply and Transport (BEST) bus network covers the entire city.

  • Metro: The Mumbai Metro provides a rapid transit system, easing commuting in certain areas.

  • Taxis and Auto-ricksaws: Widely available for convenient point-to-point travel.

  • Ferries: Connecting to the city to coastal areas and nearby islands.
